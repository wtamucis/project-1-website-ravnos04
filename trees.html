Why Trees Matter

Trees are essential... Urban trees cool neighborhoods and improve air quality.

Beyond their ecological role, trees offer inspiration. Writers, artists, and communities often center traditions around them. A simple oak or maple can become a landmark in a neighborhood.

Visiting a forest path is one of the easiest ways to reconnect with the outdoors. It’s also good exercise and a reminder of how small we are in the big picture.
